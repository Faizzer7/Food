Technical Analysis

Technical analysis is the study of historical price and volume data to forecast future price movements in financial markets. It is a methodology used by traders to evaluate and predict the behavior of financial markets based on past market data, primarily price and volume. Unlike fundamental analysis, which focuses on the underlying economic factors, technical analysis is purely concerned with price movements and patterns.

Traders use various tools in technical analysis to identify trends, reversal patterns, support and resistance levels, and potential market entries and exits. By understanding the behavior of price movements and analyzing patterns, technical analysis enables traders to make informed decisions in a wide range of markets, including forex, stocks, and commodities.
